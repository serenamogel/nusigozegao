Kathleen McCrone is an American criminal and civil attorney best known as the wife of prominent American singer and actor, Wayne Newton.

Kathleen McCrone was born on the 21st of March 1964 in Cleveland, Ohio to parents, William J. McCrone and Marilyn Furman. Her father, William was a federal judge in the Rocky River Municipal Court and Cuyahoga Court of Common Pleas, Ohio.

However, Kathleen McCrone’s father passed away from cancer. Kathleen was raised alongside two sisters, Mary and Patricia “Tricia” Clift McCrone. Sadly, Mary McCrone died from a heart attack in 2009.

Not much is known about Kathleen McCrone’s educational background. She rose into the spotlight following her marriage to famous American entertainer, Wayne Newton. The duo first met in 1990 in Las Vegas.

Kathleen McCrone and Wayne tied the knot on 9th April 1994 at Wayne Newton’s 52-acre ranch estate, the Casa de Shenandoah.
