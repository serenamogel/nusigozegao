Surender Sharma is a poet who creates humorous drawings of himself and his wife on a regular basis. He is from the hamlet of Nangal Chaudhary in Haryana’s Mahendragarh area and has made a significant contribution to Haryanvi humorous poetry. He earned his bachelor’s degree in commerce from Delhi University’s Shri Ram College of Commerce (SRCC). He also utilises the Marwari language in his comedy on occasion. His famous opening phrase for many of his poems is chaar lainaa suna ryo hoon, which translates to “I’m going to speak four lines,” and the poker-faced alter ago,” which he employed while delivering comedic material on the Kavi sammelan circuit. He is one of Hasya Ras’s first few poets (Comic and Satiric Poetry).
