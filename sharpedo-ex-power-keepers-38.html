Sharpedo (Japanese: サメハダー Samehader) is a Darkness-type Stage 1 Pokémon card. It is part of the EX Power Keepers expansion.

Card text

Release information

This card was included in the English EX Power Keepers expansion, first released as one of the Japanese PCG-P Promotional cards. In Japan, it was released as part of a Meiji Chocolate campaign in June 2006. It was later included in the Japanese World Champions Pack.
