Biography

Hayden Hurst was born in Jacksonville, FL on August 24, 1993. American professional football player is known for playing at a tight end for the Baltimore Ravens of the National Football League. He is known for having been selected in the first round and 25th overall pick in the 2018 NFL Draft. He was also the first tight end picked in the draft that year. Other tight ends known for playing with the Baltimore Ravens include Todd Heap, Ed Dickson, and Maxx Williams. He attended Bolles School in Jacksonville, Florida.
