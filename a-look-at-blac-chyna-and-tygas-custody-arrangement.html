Both of the socialite’s baby daddies reacted angrily to her claims about having to make sacrifices to raise her children due to a lack of child support.

Chyna has a 9-year-old son with Tyga, whom she dated between 2011 and 2014. She then had a whirlwind romance and engagement with Rob, with whom she shares her five-year-old daughter, Dream.

Rob Kardashian and Blac Chyna Reach New Custody Agreement

Rob Kardashian and Blac Chyna share physical custody of Dream, according to a custody agreement signed in 2020. The ex-couple has an alternating weekly schedule and agreed to split time equally during holidays and vacations.

The couple, who welcomed their daughter Dream in 2016, previously agreed to joint custody in 2017, but it didn’t work out. Rob had previously requested sole custody of their daughter. The new arrangement, however, appears to be working best for the former couple. Both parents agreed to abstain from drugs and alcohol while their child is in their care.

A look At Blac Chyna And Tyga’s Custody Arrangement

While the details of Rob and Blac Chyna’s custody arrangement are public knowledge, her relationship with former flame Tyga remains unknown.

Tyga revealed in his most recent response to the reality star’s tweets that he was caring for their son, who lives with him Monday through Saturday. His response revealed that Blac Chyna only gets King Cairo on Sunday.

Moreover, back in 2016, a Daily Mail report revealed that Tyga filed legal documents to change the custody agreement for King Cairo after Chyna was arrested in an Austin, Texas airport over drug possession and verbal aggression. The details of the former couple’s present custody arrangement have not been explicitly revealed.

“How is that disrespect when they have the kids more than she does? She should be paying them child support,” opined one fan.
