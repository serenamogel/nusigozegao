    Baked Cherry Cheesecake French Toast

    This easy Baked Cherry Cheesecake French Toast Casserole is the breakfast of your dreams! Just 5 minutes of assembly and you're ready to pop it in the oven. Breakfast has never been this delicious!

    Course Breakfast Cuisine American Keyword cheesecake, cherry, french toast casserole Prep Time 5 minutes minutes Cook Time 1 hour hour Total Time 1 hour hour 5 minutes minutes Servings 8 servings Calories 406kcal Author Trish - Mom On Timeout

    Ingredients

    • 12 oz French baguette cut into 1-inch cubes, about 5 cups
    • 8 oz cream cheese cut into 20 cubes
    • 21 oz cherry pie filling
    • 6 large eggs
    • 1/2 cup milk
    • 1/2 cup half and half
    • 1/4 cup maple syrup the real stuff!
    • 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
    • 1/4 tsp ground nutmeg
    • 1 tbsp vanilla extract

    Instructions

    • Spray an 8-inch or 9-inch square baking dish with cooking spray.

    • Place half of the bread on the bottom of the dish.

    • Place the cream cheese cubes on top of the bread.

    • Spoon half of the cherry pie filling on top of the cream cheese.

    • Top with the remaining half of the bread cubes.

    • Whisk together remaining ingredients (eggs, milk, half and half, syrup, cinnamon, nutmeg, and vanilla).

    • Pour over the top of the bread layer.

    • Spoon the remaining pie filling over the top and cover with foil.

    • Preheat oven to 350F. Let french toast sit while the oven is preheating, allowing the bread to absorb some of the egg mixture.

    • Cover with foil and bake for 45 minutes covered. Remove foil and bake for an additional 15 minutes.

    • Let cool slightly before serving with additional maple syrup if desired.
