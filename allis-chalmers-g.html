The Allis Chalmers Model G is a small implement carrier tractor that was manufactured by the Allis Chalmers Manufacturing Company.

At the time of its introduction, the Allis Chalmers Model G was unique for its rear-mounted, Continental N62 engine. It was a four-cylinder engine with a 2⅜ × 3½ inch bore and stroke.

The Allis Chalmers G Tool Carrier Tractor was designed for small farms and vegetable gardeners, and a line of implements specifically designed for it, including planters, cultivators, and plows.
