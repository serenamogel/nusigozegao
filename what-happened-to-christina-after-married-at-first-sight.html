What Happened To Christina After Married At First Sight?

April 20, 2024
blog

During Decision Day, Christina Crochet revealed she had spent extended time in Mexico after her split with Henry Rodriguez. "I'm ready to move on and live my life healthy," she said with tears in her eyes. The flight attendant also expressed regret for publicly questioning Henry's sexuality. "I've apologized to him," she said. "I acknowledge the apology," Henry replied. "It may take some time to accept it. It was just a crappy thing to do." 

At the time, fans assumed she traveled to Mexico to recollect herself following a tumultuous Married at First Sight experience. However, according to Screen Rant, Christina possibly spent time in the country to undergo cosmetic surgery. Photos of the TLC star taken after the show aired featured a seemingly slimmed-down Christina with straighter teeth, Soap Dirt reported. Of course, there's nothing wrong with a glow-up. Shine on, girl!

As for Christina's social media presence these days, she might have deactivated her Instagram account, Screen Rant noted. It also doesn't look like she's spending time with the other MAFS cast members — as of this writing, she hasn't appeared in any of their hangout pics since the reunion wrapped. Who knows, maybe Christina needs some alone time after the stress of filming — either way, she has plenty of fans out there who wish her the best.
